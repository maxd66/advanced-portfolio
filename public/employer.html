About Me

My name is Maxwell Dunn and I am a UCSD college graduate with a love for programming. For as long as I can remember, I have loved learning something new. Whether it be yoyoing, drawing, card magic, building computers, playing football, or becoming a neuroscientist, if I wanted it, I made it happen. I originally majored in Neuroscience and Physiology because I loved problem-solving, critical thinking, and helping others and because I had a love for the vast unknown. The medical field ended up not being my love and dream, but fortunately, I found a door into the career I had always dreamed of: Software Development. The amalgamation of creativity, math, critical thinking, and effort drew me in, but it was only recently I found out that it wasn’t a pipe dream and it wasn’t impossible to make that love for programming into a successful and respectable career. So after I graduated from UCSD, I acquired a Certificate in Full Stack Web Development from UCSD and developed skills in JavaScript, Express, React, Node, MongoDB, and several other frameworks and databases. And now, with my skills, work ethic, drive, and desire to learn, what I accomplish next is simply a matter of decision. Thank you so much for taking the time to visit my portfolio, and please contact me if you’re interested!
