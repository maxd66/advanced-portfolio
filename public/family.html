A Message for You

Hey there! I am so happy that you decided to drop by and thank you so much! I made this page especially for you and the rest of my family and friends because all of you have had an impact on my life and lead me to this point. Here I am, over 20 years in, and continuing my journey down the road to greatness, in part, thanks to you. I know that may seem silly to you (or maybe it doesn't), but it is true and I can prove it.

In psychology, there is a term called the spotlight effect that is used to describe the tendency humans have to exaggerate how much other humans notice them. Remember when you farted that one time, and you know the person next to you smelled it, but didn't say anything and you almost died of embarassment. Or, that time in middle school when you got that question wrong in front of the whole class. Whatever it was for you, you remember those moments for a long, long time. Even though the other people involved probably forgot them the next day. That is the spotlight effect, which really is just a microscopic view on the way human memory works. Basically, our brain remembers what is important to US, good or bad, and what we see as important is sometimes completely immemorable to others. So, even though you might not think it, you really did take part in molding me into the man I am today, and I, again, want to thank you for that.

Feel free to check out some of the work I've done below and the other pages on this site. And, before you go, fill out the form so you can leave your mark once more.
